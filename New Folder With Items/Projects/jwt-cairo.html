JWT-Cairo App:

Event App

The event hosted 3,000 guests where we took care of the whole experience from the e-invitation and going through several other experiences at the event.

Year: 2014

App Features

Users were able to scan logos of all customers that worked with JWT and through the app they were able to view the oldest ad that was made for their brand. They were also able to take pictures from the app and share them on the screens and projectors at the event to be viewed by all a]endees as well as a nice memory note to be seen by everyone. The App included AR (Augmented reality) that was made specially for this purpose with a state-of-the art image processing technology .
