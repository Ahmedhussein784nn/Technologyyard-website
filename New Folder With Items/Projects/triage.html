Triage

Medical Directory App
Saudi volunteering project The Triage Project trains volunteers in the art of healthcare clinical skills, applied using the diagnostic software Triage Application. Residents in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia are offered clinical reviews and referrals to healthcare facilities. This practice in the field will serve community participants with clinical screenings and referrals to government hospitals. The program is currently launched in Riyadh- KSA and is expected to expand to reach other places at the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Year: 2016

App Features

The Triage Project trains volunteers in the art of healthcare clinical skills, applied using the diagnostic software Triage Application. Residents of under developed communities in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia are offered clinical reviews and referrals to healthcare facilities. This practice in the field will serve community participants with clinical screenings and referrals to government hospitals. The program is currently launched in Riyadh-KSA and is expected to expand to reach other places at the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
